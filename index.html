While many theories exist to explain why we dream, no one yet fully understands their purpose. Dreams can be mysterious but understanding the meaning of our dreams can be downright baffling. Our dreams' contents can shift suddenly, feature bizarre elements, or frighten us with terrifying imagery. Though research has not demonstrated a purpose for dreams, many experts believe that dreams DO have meaning. Dream analysis can be a fun way to learn more about your mental and emotional wellness.
Find out more on verywellmind.

How to Begin Interpreting Your Dreams:

  1. Think about starting a dream journal.
  2. Share your dreams with someone.
  3. Try to identify symbols and associations.
  4. Every day, strive to remember your dreams.
